_A list of data and tech projects around law, policy and advocacy in the times of Covid-19.  We wanted to curate this information at one place and share these initiatives with a wider audience, mainly for inspiration, collaboration and learning. A live version of this list can also be accessed [here](https://bit.ly/justicehub-covid)_

| Title                                                        | Category           | Description                                                  | Group                                                 |
| ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| [COVID-19 Housing Policy Scorecard](https://evictionlab.org/covid-policy-scorecard/) | Eviction           | To better understand the steps states have taken to prevent homelessness during and after the pandemic, the Eviction Lab and Columbia Law  School’s Professor Emily Benfer have developed a policy scorecard for  each state, distilling the contents of thousands of newly-released  emergency orders, declarations, and legislation into a clear set of  critical measures included in, and left out of, state-level pandemic  responses related to eviction and housing. | Eviction Lab                                                 |
| [Anti-Eviction Mapping Project](https://antievictionmappingproject.github.io/covid-19-map/) | Eviction           | Mapping states, counties, and municipalities that are enacting a moratorium on evictions due to the corona virus pandemic. | Anti Eviction Mapping Project                                |
| [Tracking the statements of Departments of Corrections on the COVID-19 virus](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1nZe02Py77gtnyNxHO7Bebr_wKYMKAe4ZWaoG-NKlT8Q/edit?usp=sharing) | Prison Policy      | A spreadsheet showing what each state Department of Corrections has told the public | Prison Policy Initiative                                     |
| [UCLA Law Covid-19 Behind Bars Data Project](https://law.ucla.edu/centers/criminal-justice/criminal-justice-program/related-programs/covid-19-behind-bars-data-project/) | Prison Policy      | The project tracks Covid-19 conditions in jails and prisons and the efforts — both in and out of court — to decrease jail and prison populations and improve conditions to ensure the safety of residents and staff. | UCLA Prison Law and  Policy Program                          |
| [COVID-19 and Prisoners](https://www.jpp.org.pk/covid19-prisoners/) | Prison Policy      | Facts, figures and responses of Pakistan and other countries towards protecting vulnerable prisoners in the wake of the coronavirus pandemic | Justice Project Pakistan                                     |
| [Coronavirus Tracker: How Justice Systems Are Responding in Each State](https://www.themarshallproject.org/2020/03/17/tracking-prisons-response-to-coronavirus) | Prisons and Courts | How Justice Systems Are Responding in Each State             | The Marshall Project                                         |
| [Criminal Justice Responses to the Coronavirus Pandemic](https://www.vera.org/projects/covid-19-criminal-justice-responses/covid-19-data) | Incarcerations     | Monitoring jail populations during COVID-19                  | Vera Institute of Justice                                    |
| [COVID-19 Fines and Fees Policy Tracker](https://finesandfeesjusticecenter.org/articles/ffjc-policy-recommendations-for-the-covid-19-crisis/) | Criminal Justice   | Fines and Fees Reform Tracking Tool                          | Fines & Fees Justice Center                                  |
| [Brennan Center Court Tracking Tool](https://www.brennancenter.org/our-work/research-reports/courts-responses-covid-19-crisis?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=socialmedia) | Criminal Justice   | Courts’ Responses to the Covid-19 Crisis                     | Brennan Center                                               |
| [The Coronavirus Response: Spotlight on State & Local Governments](https://theappeal.org/political-report/coronavirus-response-state-local/) | Government Policy  | Tracks and maps state and local government responses to COVID-19 | The Appeal Political Report                                  |
| [Resource Guide: Prisons, Policing, and Punishment](https://medium.com/@micahherskind/resource-guide-prisons-policing-and-punishment-effb5e0f6620) | Prison Policy      | [Resource Guide: Prisons, Policing, and Punishment, by Micah Herskind](https://medium.com/@micahherskind/resource-guide-prisons-policing-and-punishment-effb5e0f6620) | [Micah Herskind](https://medium.com/@micahherskind?source=post_page-----effb5e0f6620----------------------) |
| [Jail Population](http://end-incarceration.org/)             | Prison Policy      | Up to Date prison and jail population nationwide - can select jurisdiction and state and includes links to various other resources | End Incarceration                                            |
| [Covid Prison Data](https://covidprisondata.com/)            | Prison Policy      | This site offers the following data: Number of incarcerated people tested for Covid-19 in correctional facilities by state, Number of incarcerated people who tested positive for Covid-19 in correctional facilities by state, Number of incarcerated people who tested negative for Covid-19 in correctional facilities by state, Historical population data for state prisons, Web scrapers built to extract data from Department of Corrections dashboards. | [Covid Prison Data](https://covidprisondata.com/about.html)  |
| [Taft - Coronavirus (COVID-19) Resource Toolkit](https://www.taftlaw.com/general/coronavirus-covid-19-resource-toolkit?platform=hootsuite) | Government Policy  | Taft’s multi-disciplinary COVID-19 Toolkit - includes some focus on employmenet policies (unemployment insurance, paid sick leave, paid family leave) and shifts in response to COVID-19 | TaftLaw                                                      |
| [Bank Industry - Response to the Corona Virus](https://www.aba.com/about-us/press-room/industry-response-coronavirus#AlphaB) | Economic Security  | Tracking bank responses to the coronavirus.                  | American Bankers Association                                 |
| [COVID-19 Digital Rights Tracker](https://www.top10vpn.com/news/surveillance/covid-19-digital-rights-tracker/) | Digital Rights     | COVID-19 Digital Rights Tracker                              | Top10VPN                                                     |
